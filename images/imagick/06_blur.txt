
//Blur - reduce image noise and reduce detail levels
//-blur geometry
convert original.jpg -blur 0x5 result.jpeg
display result.jpeg

//Adaptive Blur - adaptively blur pixels; decrease effect near edges
//-adaptive-blur geometry
convert original.jpg -adaptive-blur 0x5 result.jpeg
display result.jpeg


//Geometry
 - radius x sigma
 - чем больше sigma, тем больше blur


//Other blur
 -motion-blur geometry - simulate motion blur
 -radial-blur angle - radial blur the image
 -selective-blur geometry - selectively blur pixels within a contrast threshold


//deblur (sharpen)
 -sharpen geometry - sharpen the image
 -unsharp geometry - sharpen the image
 -adaptive-sharpen geometry - adaptively sharpen pixels; increase effect near edges
